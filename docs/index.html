Import creators from CSV

Paste rows or choose a .csv file. Include a header row — columns name, handle, email, market, subs, niche are matched automatically (order doesn't matter). Creators already on your roster (matching handle, email or name) are skipped.

Add creators from YouTube links

Paste YouTube channel links — one per line. Any form works: youtube.com/@handle, /channel/UC…, a custom URL, or even a video link. We auto-fill name, handle, subscribers & market. YouTube hides creator emails, so those come back blank — add them in the roster (click the email cell). Duplicates already on your roster are skipped. Up to 40 at a time.
